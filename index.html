About Us

The World's Best Physiotherapy Clinic That You Can Trust

S.P.O.R.T. has been owned and managed by Dr. Vinod Kumar Jain who is a physiotherapist by profession. Dr.Vinod collectively brings over 14 years of experience to the practice as both – a manual “hands-on” therapist, as well as a sport rehabilitation specialist.

After graduating with a Bachelor of Physiotherapy from the Tamil Nadu University, Dr. Vinod has gained experience with working with a number of hospitals, private practices, as well as sports clubs at national and international level. With experience in treating musculoskeletal conditions and sport injuries, Dr. Vinod has also specialized in Dry Needling techniques and acupuncture. More recently Vinod has specialized further in Occupational Health. He has done extensive work in the field of ergonomics and treating work/overuse related injuries, providing personalized workstation assessments and completing worksite visits. The well-known personalities that Dr. Vinod has treated include the Former Prime Minister of India Mr. Deve Gowda, as well as Indian cricket legends like former Indian captain Rahul Dravid as well as ace spinner Anil Kumble.

Manual therapy performed by the sensitive hands of our highly trained physiotherapists can reduce pain and drastically increase strength. Being an IT professional, I suffered from severe RSI (repetitive strain injury) for over 4 months. Constant shoulder pain and shooting pain down the neck and all the way to my right hand were some of the symptoms that i suffered from. I consulted multiple doctors & physiotherapists across the city, who gave me different treatments and prescribed various medication. But even after 4 months, there was no improvement. I was recommended to see Dr. Vinod Jain by my sister, who had a successful experience. Dr. Vinod spent an hour getting to know me, my lifestyle and my history. Based on the provided information, he diagnosed my condition as severe RSI. Not only did he diagnose me, like other doctors, but also patiently went through a detailed anatomical explanation of the issue, and causes and prevention. Post this, he first started with Manual Therapy for a few sessions where he mobilized some of the stiff areas, and then later moved on to few sessions with ultra sound treatment. Post this, we graduated to strength training towards the end of the treatment. Now pain-free with all these treatments, my belief in Dr. Jain and his method of practice has increased tremendously, and I am referring to everyone I know to the structure of this mode of practice.
